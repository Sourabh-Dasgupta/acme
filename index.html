About Us

About Us

ACME Beacon School grooms the students from PG-VIII at Govind Nagar and Acme Institute caters the needs of students from IX-XII at Sampoornanand Nagar Sigra under the aegis of ACME Educational and Welfare Society.

ACME Beacon School is a place of excellence, which is child focused and friendly where everyone can recognise and achieve their full potential in a happy and safe environment. ACME Beacon School is a school from Play group to Grade VIII recently opened in 2016 under the aegis of ACME Educational and Welfare Trust. Acme Institute serves to cater the needs of children from classes VIII to XII.

Our vision is to inspire every child for life through a rounded education of a child's body, mind, heart and spirit to help them grow into a complete human being. We welcome you all to be a golden link in this chain.

F.A.Q

  • What is the Eligibility Criteria for Admission?

    A student seeking admission to any class in a 'School' will be eligible for admission to that Class only if he :

    1. has been studying in a School recognised by or affiliated to this Board or any other recognised Board of Secondary Education in India.
    2. has passed qualifying or equivalent qualifying examination making him eligible for admission to that Class.
    3. atisfies the requirements of age limits (minimum and maximum) as determined by the State/U. T. Government and applicable to the place where the School is located.
    4. Produces:
      1. The School Leaving Certificate/Transfer Certificate signed by the Head of the Institution last attended and countersigned, if required as provided elsewhere, in these Byelaws.
      2. Document(s) in support of his having passed the qualifying or equivalent qualifying examination and
      3. *Date of Birth Certificate issued by the Registrar of Birth and Deaths, where-ever existing,as proof of date of birth.

  • What curriculum will the school follow?

    School follows CBSE curriculum

  • What will be the school timings?

    Classes PG to UKG : 8:00am to 1:00pm
    Classes I to VIII : 8:00am to 2:00pm
